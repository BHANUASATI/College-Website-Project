About Our Principal

It is a matter of great privilege that I have got the opportunity to perform my duties, as Principal of the prestigious 131-year-old, Government Holkar (Model Autonomous) Science College. The institution was established on 10th June 1891, by His Highness Maharaja Shri Shivajirao Holkar, the erstwhile ruler of Indore. The college has been accredited with ‘A’ grade by NAAC on 19th January 2016, with CGPA 3.23. It is one of the largest Government Science Colleges in the state. The college works under the auspices of the Department of Higher Education, Government of MP and is affiliated to Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, Indore. We wish to develop this college as a premier Research Centre of Science and Technology, that is ranked among the top most institutions of the country and an Alma Mater, par excellence. As Principal, I have a strong desire to establish harmony, equality and an intellectual atmosphere for everyone on the campus. It is my firm belief, that with the cooperation, hard work and dedication of our professors, students and office- staff, we can fulfill the vision and mission of the institution. I wish good luck and grand success to all the students for their future endeavours. In Swami Vivekanand's words “We want that education by which character is formed, strength of mind is increased, the intellect is expanded, and by which one can stand on one's own feet.”
